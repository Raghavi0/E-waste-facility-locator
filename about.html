About Us




The Electronic Waste (E-Waste) Facility Locator provides a comprehensive database of electronic waste collection facilities across the United States. The database is maintained by the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) and includes information on facilities that accept household hazardous waste, including electronics such as computers, televisions, and cell phones. To find an E-Waste facility near you, you can visit the EPA's website and use their " Household Hazardous Waste Collection Facility Search" tool. Simply enter your location or zip code, and the tool will provide a list of nearby facilities that accept electronic waste. You can also filter the results by facility type and hours of operation.



Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the locator tool?

Simply enter your location in the search bar and click 'Search' to find nearby e-waste facilities.

What types of e-waste can I recycle?

You can recycle a variety of electronic items including computers, phones, appliances, and more. Check with the specific facility for detailed information.

Why is e-waste recycling important?

Recycling e-waste helps prevent harmful materials from entering the environment and allows for the recovery of valuable materials.
